========================================================================
                     ZENITH SOLUTIONS - COMPANY GUIDELINES
========================================================================

Welcome to Zenith Solutions! This document outlines key organizational 
information, remote work benefits, and core product descriptions.

------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. ABOUT THE COMPANY
------------------------------------------------------------------------
Zenith Solutions was founded in October 2018 by Dr. Clara Mercer, a 
pioneer in distributed computing systems. The company's global headquarters 
is located in Seattle, Washington, with satellite offices in Munich, Germany 
and Tokyo, Japan. Our mission is to democratize secure machine learning 
operations for small and medium-sized enterprises.

------------------------------------------------------------------------
2. EMPLOYEE BENEFITS & PERKS
------------------------------------------------------------------------
At Zenith Solutions, we support a healthy work-life balance by providing 
the following standard employee perks:
- Wellness Stipend: An uncapped wellness allowance of $150 per month, 
  which can be used for gym memberships, therapy sessions, or sporting gear.
- Volunteer Leave: Employees are granted four weeks (20 business days) of 
  fully paid volunteer leave per calendar year to support nonprofit organizations.
- Workspace Allowance: An annual stipend of $500 to purchase ergonomic office 
  furniture, monitor setups, or keyboard peripherals for remote work.

------------------------------------------------------------------------
3. SOFTWARE PRODUCTS & SERVICES
------------------------------------------------------------------------
Zenith Solutions develops and maintains three proprietary enterprise systems:

A. Aegis Cloud Security
A zero-trust cloud protection system that runs real-time network anomaly 
detection using federated learning. It is currently deployed across over 
400 enterprise clients globally.

B. Horizon Data Analytics
An analytics dashboard that processes large streams of IoT data, providing 
predictive maintenance schedules with an accuracy rate of 98.4%.

C. Apex Machine Learning Engine
A serverless ML deployment suite that lets software engineers compile, containerize, 
and deploy PyTorch and TensorFlow models to production clusters in under 
three minutes.
